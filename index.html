About Me

My name is Kevin Smith. My fitness journey started 10 years ago. I started by taking a personal training course and fell in love with it immediately. I completed this level 7 course and specialised in Nutrition. My aim is to educate my clients from what I have learned over the past 10 years to help them achieve their fitness goals. My goal is to show the world how adopting a few healthy eating and exercise habits will enhance people's lives physically and mentally.
